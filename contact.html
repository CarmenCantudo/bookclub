What our members think

It is a wonderful experience, to have a space where we can meet to sharelaughter, experiences, points of view, opinions and discussions that always start with the theme of the book that we have chosen and thatend with some fun games, with talks about how our day has gone and especially with an excellent company.

The pandemic has consolidated us. For us, the meeting every two weeks is our group therapy. A small family has been formed.

A book club with friends I love is a break from my routine: something other than my children, work, husband, home. It is a space that I dedicate to myself. That's why I read with enthusiasm.

I had never been to a book club before, but it caught my attention because although I love to read, since my son was born, I had not touched a book. And I started the year with the purpose of changing that.
